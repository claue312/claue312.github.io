    Simon's Gift

    Alisha's husband, Simon, has his birthday tomorrow. She wants to give him an special gift, but he seems to already have it all...

    Alisha calls her best friend, Sveta, to consult about gift giving. After much speculation, Sveta recommends giving him an experiential gift. She knows of a great day spa just 30 minutes shy of Hidden Springs. "I actually need to run an errand in that direction...I could pick up a spa certicate if you don't mind watching Wayne!" explains Sveta. Alisha pauses and contemplates. She's flattered by Sveta's offer, but hesitates because of her hatred of young children.

    Alisha agrees to take care of Sveta's baby. She walks three doors down to Sveta's house. She explains that the baby is in the crib upstairs, sleeping. Alisha goes into Sveta's house to find the child.
